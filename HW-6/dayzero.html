Day Zero-

The last day of the world created by man and the first day of the world survived by him. Can you survive the onslaught of the infected? walkers hell bent on one thing; feeding.

Day Zero is a first person text/decision based game that will take you through a dangerzone zombies, death, and survival.
Just you, your survival, and a buttload of zombies. Do you have what it takes to make the hard decisions?

Can you survive what is coming?
What will your ultimate fate be as you take your place in this new world of the strife, brains, and the undead?
